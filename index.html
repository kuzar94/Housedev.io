L&Q at Ridgeway Views offers a collection 1, 2 & 3 bedroom Shared Ownership apartments in the North London suburb of Barnet. These homes are anticipated to launch in September this year. Across several blocks, these homes are well positioned for those who enjoy being surrounded by natural beauty but work in the city. With picturesque rolling hills in abundance and connections to the city from Mill Hill East Underground and Mill Hill East Broadway, this is the perfect balance to cater to both your country pleasures and city needs. It is easy to forget that you are still in London.
